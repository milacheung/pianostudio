# Children's Privacy (COPPA)

## Our Commitment to Children's Privacy

PianoStudio is committed to protecting the privacy of children. This section explains our practices regarding children under 13 years old, in compliance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

## Parental Consent Requirement

**We do not allow children under 13 to create accounts directly.**

For children under 13:

1. A parent or guardian must create the child's account
2. The parent must provide verifiable consent before any data is collected
3. The child's account is linked to and controlled by the parent's account

## Verifiable Parental Consent

We obtain verifiable parental consent through:

- **Physical consent form** signed by the parent and provided to the studio teacher, OR
- **Electronic consent** with identity verification during account creation

Consent covers:

- Collection of child's first name and practice data
- Display of child's first name to other studio members
- Child's participation in the studio feed (posting, reactions)
- Photos of the child visible to other studio members (if applicable)

## Information Collected from Children

We collect only information necessary for participation:

| Information | Purpose | Required? |
|-------------|---------|-----------|
| First name | Display in app | Yes |
| Practice data | Progress tracking | Yes |
| Posts/reactions | Community participation | Optional |
| Profile photo | Identification | Optional |

We do NOT collect from children:

- Full name (only first name displayed)
- Address or precise location
- Phone number
- School name
- Any information beyond what's needed for the service

## Parental Rights

Parents have the right to:

### Review Information

Request to see all personal information collected from your child by contacting [INSERT EMAIL].

### Delete Information

Request deletion of your child's personal information. We will delete the data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

### Refuse Further Collection

Withdraw consent for further collection of your child's information. Note: This may require closing the child's account.

### Manage Account

- View all activity on your child's account
- Delete posts made by your child
- Modify your child's profile
- Close your child's account

## How to Exercise Parental Rights

Contact us at [INSERT EMAIL] with:

- Your name and email (matching the parent account)
- Your child's name/account
- The action you wish to take

We will verify your identity before processing requests.

## Teacher's Role

The studio teacher:

- May view all student data for educational purposes
- Moderates the studio feed
- Does not have authority to consent on behalf of parents
- Reports concerns to parents

## Data Security for Children

We implement additional protections for children's data:

- No direct messaging to/from children
- All child posts visible to linked parent
- Teacher moderation of all content
- No public profiles (studio members only)

# Photo and Media Consent

## Overview

PianoStudio allows users to share photos and videos within their studio community. This section explains how media featuring individuals (especially children) is handled.

## Types of Media

| Media Type | Who Can Post | Who Can See |
|------------|--------------|-------------|
| Profile photo | Account owner / Parent | Studio members |
| Practice videos | Student / Parent | Studio members |
| Achievement posts | Student / Parent | Studio members |
| Recital photos | Teacher / Parents | Studio members |
| Group photos | Teacher | Studio members |

## Consent for Minors

**No photos or videos of minors may be posted without parental consent.**

Parents provide consent for their child's image to appear in the app via:

- The Parent/Guardian Consent Form (below), OR
- Electronic consent during account setup

## What Consent Covers

By providing media consent, you agree that:

- Your child's image may appear in photos/videos posted by the teacher or other parents
- Your child may post photos/videos of themselves (subject to your oversight)
- These images are visible to other studio members (not public)
- Images are stored securely via Cloudinary

## Opting Out

You may opt out of media sharing:

- **During signup:** Decline media consent on the consent form
- **After signup:** Contact [INSERT EMAIL] to update preferences

If you opt out:

- Your child will not appear in group photos posted to the feed
- Your child cannot post photos/videos
- Practice tracking and other features remain available

## Removal Requests

To request removal of a photo/video containing your child:

1. Contact the teacher directly, OR
2. Email [INSERT EMAIL] with a description of the content
3. We will remove the content within 48 hours

## Prohibited Content

The following media is prohibited:

- Photos/videos of children without parental consent
- Inappropriate or revealing images
- Content showing identifiable locations (home addresses, schools)
- Content showing non-members without consent
- Any content violating our Terms of Service

## Third-Party Storage

Media is stored via Cloudinary, a third-party service. By uploading media, you acknowledge:

- Media is transmitted to and stored by Cloudinary
- Cloudinary's privacy policy applies to stored media
- We retain control over access permissions
